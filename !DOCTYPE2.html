My Story: From Curiosity to Code

My journey into technology began not in a big tech hub, but in the heart of Zambia. Growing up, my first spark of curiosity came in junior secondary school: I couldn't stop wondering how everything I saw on a computer actually worked.

That curiosity quickly evolved into a focused passion. Watching my uncle—a Computer Science graduate—code and solve real-world problems using C++ became my biggest inspiration. It showed me that programming was more than just syntax; it was a powerful tool for creation.

Today, I’m pursuing my Bachelor's in Software Engineering at United States International University – Africa (USIU-A). My technical foundation is strong, covering essential web languages like JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, as well as Python and C++. I've built this knowledge through a blend of formal courses, self-study, and mentorship.

I don't just write code; I aim to find innovative solutions that make a difference. My projects reflect this drive: I've successfully developed and launched a website for my fashion design business and created a practical tax calculation program to simplify financial planning for users. While coding is often challenging, I approach every obstacle with patience, positivity, and persistence, which continually sharpens my problem-solving skills.

Likes & Inspirations

I'm fundamentally inspired by the relentless pace of technological evolution and its constant demand for critical and creative thinking.
